Maintain and Upgrade Your UPVC Doors

Regular servicing extends the life of your doors and allows for security upgrades such as anti-snap cylinders.

Expert Adjustments and Realignments

Seasonal temperature changes and frequent use can cause doors to drop or warp. Our technicians realign frames, adjust hinges and re-pack double glazing to ensure your door closes smoothly and seals properly against drafts.

We also lubricate moving parts and replace worn rollers or keeps to keep your multi-point locking system operating reliably.

Energy Efficiency and Security Upgrades

Older uPVC doors often lack modern energy-saving features. We can fit new weather seals, install energy-efficient glazing and upgrade locks to meet current security standards. Anti-snap cylinders and reinforced handles protect against forced entry, while letterbox restrictors and sash jammers offer extra peace of mind.
