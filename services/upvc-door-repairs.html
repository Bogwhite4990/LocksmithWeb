UPVC Door Repairs

Specialist UPVC Lock and Mechanism Repairs

UPVC doors and windows have complex multi-point locking mechanisms that can fail over time. If your door is difficult to lock, your handle is floppy, or the key won't turn, it's time to call an expert. We specialize in all aspects of UPVC door and window lock repairs.

We can often repair mechanisms, saving you the cost of a full replacement. Our services include:

  • UPVC door and window lock repairs
  • Mechanism and gearbox replacement
  • Handle and cylinder replacement
  • Door realignment to fix sticking or catching
  • Security upgrades for UPVC doors
