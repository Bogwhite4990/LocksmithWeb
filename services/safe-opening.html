Non-Destructive Opening Techniques

Whenever possible, we use picking methods, manipulation and decoding tools that preserve the integrity of your safe. If drilling is unavoidable, our engineers keep holes to a minimum and restore the unit to its original security rating after opening.

Once open, we can service hinges, replace worn bolts and supply new keys or codes so your safe is ready for continued use.
