Tips to Avoid Future Lockouts

To reduce the chance of a lockout, keep a spare key in a safe place and replace fob batteries annually. A quick check that your keys are in hand before shutting the boot can save an emergency call-out. We can provide duplicate keys and coded spares during the visit to help you stay prepared.
