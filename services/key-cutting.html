Advanced Cutting Technology

Our workshop is equipped with state-of-the-art key cutting machines that replicate even the most intricate security keys. Laser-cut car keys, dimple keys and tubular keys are all produced with millimetre accuracy to guarantee a perfect fit.

We stock blanks for thousands of lock brands, allowing us to cut spares for rare or imported locks that many shops cannot handle. Each key is hand-finished and deburred to ensure smooth operation.

Mobile Key Cutting at Your Doorstep

Can't make it to our shop? Our mobile service brings professional key duplication to your home or business. Ideal for landlords managing multiple properties, we can cut large batches of keys on-site and label them for easy distribution.

Vehicle owners benefit from our van-based key programming units. We can clone transponder chips, program remote fobs and cut mechanical keys for most makes and models while you wait.

Why Spare Keys Matter

Having a spare key saves time and money when keys are misplaced or locked inside. It also reduces wear on your original key, prolonging the life of both the key and the lock. We recommend creating duplicates for family members, trusted neighbours and employees who require regular access.

For added security, ask about our restricted key profiles that prevent unauthorised duplication. Only registered users can order copies, giving you complete control over who holds keys to your property.

Whatever your requirements, our key cutting specialists provide friendly advice and rapid service. Visit us or schedule a mobile appointment to keep your property secure with accurately cut spare keys.
