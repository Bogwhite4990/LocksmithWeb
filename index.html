Frequently Asked Questions

Ask Us Anything

What should I do if I'm locked out of my house?

Stay calm and give us a call. Our emergency locksmiths are available 24/7 and can typically reach you within 30 minutes to get you back inside safely and without damage to your property.

How long does it take for a locksmith to arrive?

We pride ourselves on our fast response times. For emergency callouts within London, we aim to arrive at your location within 15-30 minutes of your call.

Can you make a new key without the original?

Yes, in most cases, we can create a new key for you even if you don't have the original. We can cut keys for most types of locks, including standard door locks, car locks, and high-security locks.

Is it possible to have all my locks work with one key?

Absolutely. This is called keying alike. We can rekey your existing compatible locks or install new locks that are keyed to a single key for your convenience.

What are British Standard locks and why should I have them?

British Standard (BS3621) locks are high-security locks that have been tested against common burglary techniques. Many insurance companies require them, and they provide a significantly higher level of security for your home or business.

Do you offer a guarantee on your work?

Yes, we do. All parts we fit come with a 12-month manufacturer's guarantee, and our workmanship is fully guaranteed. Your peace of mind is our priority.
